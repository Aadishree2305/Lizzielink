Suspects of Murders.

Prime Suspect: Lizzie Borden. Lizzie became the prime suspect and a few days later the police issued an order to arrest her. The woman declared herself innocent ,but was arrested until her trial 10 months later.
Second Suspect: Bridget Sullivan. She was also suspected because she was home, and took stand for Lizzie at the court.
Third Suspect: John Morse. He arrived at the evening of August 3rd, but never came back on August 4th of evening.
